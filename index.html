Pad Kraphao

The most popular Thai street food(even more than Pad Thai!) this menu include meat as main ingredient. Including herb as Thai Basil and pounded chili with garlic. Seasoning with fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce.

Soi Ju

The Infamous and exotic menu from Thai Northeastern region. It came with raw meat and organs, with spicy cow dung dip that gives you a bitter and very hot flavour. Very great menu if you bring some beer to have with!

Fermented fish papaya salad

You'll love this amazing Fermented fish papaya salad! This food is originally from Laos and it's very popular in every Thai people who's love spicy and heavy flavoured food. If you like something taste really heavy, this one is a go to choice!
